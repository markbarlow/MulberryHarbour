Mulberry Harbour

Warm, organic and eclectic electronic music and soundtracks

Learn More Learn More

Soundtracks

fsdfsdf

Mulberry Harbour produces soundtrack music combining the warmth, phrasing and individuality of live recording with electronica, samples and programming. As well as producing specifically commissioned music, a large number of existing tracks are available for licensing.

Mulberry Harbour writes soundtrack music primarily in the following styles:

  • Acoustic
  • Electronica
  • Neoclassical/Orchestral
  • Ambient
  • Folk
  • Rock/Indie
