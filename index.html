Welcome


Mulberry Harbour is an electronic musician and soundtrack composer based in London, UK, with noteable acoustic, modern classical and Hispanic influences, and a melodic sound which tilts between beauty and reverie, hope and tristesse.

Check out the streams on the right for instrumental tracks featuring electronica, acoustic guitars, cuatro, theremin, musical saw, charango, electric guitar, rhodes keyboards, violins and a whole lot more.
